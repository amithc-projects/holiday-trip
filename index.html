map Interactive Day-by-Day Route

Select any day to display its path and focus the map on key sites.

Day 1
home Base: Tbilisi

Arrive in Tbilisi

Arrive at Tbilisi International Airport (TBS) from London. Check into your luxury Old Town apartment, relax, and enjoy your first Georgian dinner with local wine.

Flight Transfer Tbilisi
Day 2
home Base: Tbilisi

Explore Tbilisi Old Town

Spend the day walking through Old Tbilisi. Visit the Narikala Fortress via cable car, relax in the Sulphur Baths, explore modern Shardeni Street, and drive up to the massive Chronicles of Georgia monument.

Sightseeing Historic Baths Chronicles
Day 3
home Base: Tbilisi

Excursion to Mtskheta

Travel to the ancient capital of Georgia. Visit the hilltop Jvari Monastery (stunning confluence of Aragvi and Mtkvari rivers) and the Svetitskhoveli Cathedral, a focal point of Georgian Orthodoxy.

Mtskheta UNESCO Short Drive
Day 4
home Base: Tbilisi

Uplistsikhe Cave Town & Gori

Explore the fascinating ancient rock-hewn town of Uplistsikhe, dating back to the early Iron Age. On the return trip, stop in Gori to optionally visit the museum of Joseph Stalin.

Cave Town History Gori
Day 5
home Base: Tbilisi

Armenia Monastery Day Trip

An organized excursion across the Sadakhlo border. Explore the stunning UNESCO World Heritage site monasteries of Haghpat and Sanahin in the Debed Canyon region of northern Armenia. Return to Tbilisi in the evening.

Armenia Organized Tour Monasteries
Day 6
home Base: Tbilisi

Free day in Tbilisi

Enjoy a relaxed day. We recommend checking out the Dry Bridge Flea Market, booking a local food and wine tasting tour, or visiting the National Gallery.

Leisure Food Tour Flea Market
Day 7
home Base: Kazbegi

Drive the Georgian Military Highway

Head north towards the High Caucasus. Stop at the turquoise Zhinvali Reservoir, explore the medieval Ananuri Fortress, cross the Jvari Pass, and visit the Gudauri Friendship Monument. Reach Stepantsminda.

Mountain Drive Fortress Gudauri
Day 8
home Base: Kazbegi

Gergeti Trinity Church Hike/4x4

Ascend to the iconic Gergeti Trinity Church, perched dramatically at 2,170 meters against the backdrop of Mount Kazbek (5,054m). Choose to hike up or hire a local 4x4.

Hiking Holy Site Gergeti
Day 9
home Base: Kazbegi

Hike in Juta Valley

Take a short drive to the remote village of Juta and hike into the valley. Marvel at the dramatic, jagged peaks of Mt. Chaukhi, often called the "Dolomites of Georgia".

Alpine Hike Scenery Chaukhi
Day 10
home Base: Baku

Return to Tbilisi & Fly to Baku

Drive back down to Tbilisi. Head straight to the airport for your evening flight to Baku, Azerbaijan (1h 10m). Check into your central premium apartment in Baku.

Drive Flight Baku
Day 11
home Base: Baku

Explore Baku Inner City (Icherisheher)

Explore Icherisheher (Old Town). Visit the Maiden Tower, the Palace of the Shirvanshahs, and modern architectural marvels like the Heydar Aliyev Centre and the Flame Towers.

Baku City Maiden Tower Shirvanshahs
Day 12
home Base: Baku

Absheron Peninsula Fire Tour

Explore the land of fire. Visit Ateshgah Fire Temple, a historic place of worship for Zoroastrians and Hindus, and Yanar Dag (Burning Mountain), where natural gas vents burn constantly.

Absheron Fire Temple Yanar Dag
Day 13
home Base: Baku

Gobustan Mud Volcanoes & Rock Art

Visit Gobustan National Park to see ancient petroglyphs (rock carvings dating back 40,000 years). Then, drive to the bizarre, bubbling mud volcanoes nearby.

Petroglyphs Mud Volcanoes Caspian Coast
Day 14
flight_land Return to London

Fly Baku to London

Enjoy your final morning in Baku strolling along the Seaside Boulevard. Board your direct afternoon flight from Heydar Aliyev International Airport (GYD) back to London.

Flight Direct Return Home

assignment_turned_in Practical Information & Logistics

gavel UK Visa Requirements

  • Georgia: Visa-free for up to 1 year.
  • Armenia: Visa-free for up to 180 days.
  • Azerbaijan: ASAN e-Visa required. Apply online 1-2 weeks prior. Takes 3 business days, costs ~$26.

directions_bus Armenia Day Excursion

To cross borders between Georgia and Armenia seamlessly, we recommend booking a private guided excursion with an established operator like Gamarjoba Georgia Tours. They supply:

  • Professional driver & crossing assistance.
  • Guided tours of Haghpat & Sanahin.
  • Homemade lunch at a local Armenian estate.

currency_exchange Currencies

  • Georgia: Georgian Lari (GEL)
  • Armenia: Armenian Dram (AMD)
  • Azerbaijan: Azerbaijani Manat (AZN)
  • Tip: Card payments are widespread in Tbilisi and Baku, but cash is essential in Kazbegi and Armenia.
